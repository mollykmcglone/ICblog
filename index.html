About Me

My name is Inspector Clouseau, aka The Inspector, aka Chief Inspector Jacques Clouseau, aka "Clouseau!".

I live in Portland, Oregon where I enjoy long walks in the rain and napping on the couch with some Netflix.

January 22nd, 2016

Hello reader, and thanks for visiting my blog. Let me start by assuring you that I know it's a little late to get into blogging. I'm aware that most of the world has moved on to Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram, where instead of long meandering blog postings you can easily consume 140-character soundbytes or simply float through image after image of kittens behaving badly. I confess that I'm rather a late-bloomer, however, and so am kicking off my "online presence", as the kids say, with this site. Of course I'll try to keep my dispatches brief, in recognition that your attention-span is under constant attack from the dark side. I hope this project will help me channel my creative energy and connect with a few like-minded creatures along the way. And so, let us begin!
